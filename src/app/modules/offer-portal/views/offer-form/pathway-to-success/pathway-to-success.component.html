Section 1 (New Offerers Only)

You are required to complete the “Pathway to Success” training and the “Readiness Assessment” located at www.gsa.gov/masroadmap. The Pathway to Success training provides an overview of the GSA Schedule, the offer process and required information, and considerations you need to make as you are developing your offer. The Readiness Assessment is a series of questions that helps you determine the readiness of your company to become a successful GSA Schedule contractor.


Section 2 (Existing FSS Contractors Only)

If you are an existing FSS contract holder and this is a streamlined offer you are not required to complete the Pathway to Success and the Readiness Assessment.

Complete Section 1 if you are a new offeror OR complete Section 2 if you are an existing FSS contractor submitting a streamlined offer.
