Contract offers and modifications made easy.

The purpose of eOffer is to create an interactive, secure electronic environment that simplifies the contracting process from submission of offers to awards. eOffer enables a seamless transmission of data from the industry community to the FAS contracting offices. In addition, eMod streamlines and expedites the modification process and creates an electronic modification for the contract file.
