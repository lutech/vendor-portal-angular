  • Open Contract Opportunities

    Contract opportunities are procurement notices from federal contracting offices. Opportunities include pre-solicitation notices, solicitation notices, award notices, and sole source notices.

    The federal government can buy cost-effective, innovative solutions for information technology (IT) requirements through Governmentwide Acquisition Contracts (GWAC) under pre-competed, multiple-award, indifinite delivery, indefinite quantity contacts.
