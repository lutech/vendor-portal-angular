Updating GSA Advantage Catalog Files

To be in full compliance with the MAS Consolidation Mass Mod A812 and avoid removal of your catalog from GSA Advantage , all contractors must complete these two important steps:

  1. Update your catalog in GSA Advantage.
  2. Update your Price List (Text File).

Please take the steps outlined in the below resources to ensure your catalog status or access to eTools is not disrupted.

  • MAS Consolidation Price List/Text File Updated Guidance
  • MAS Consolidation SIP/EDI Updated Guidance
