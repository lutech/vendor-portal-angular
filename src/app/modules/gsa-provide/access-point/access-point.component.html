Announcements

SAM

As of April 4, 2022, the government will no longer use the Data Universal Numbering System (DUNS) number as the authoritative Unique Entity Identifier (UEI) and transition to using the System for Award Management (SAM)-assigned UEI. Click here for more information on the government transition to UEI (SAM).

Vendor Portal

Starting March 8, 2021, contractors must use FAS ID multi-factor authentication to access eOffer/eMod. For more information about the implementation of FAS ID for eOffer/eMod, please visit GSA Interact.

eBuy

Starting in October 2020, contractors will use their GSA FAS ID email address, instead of the contract number to log in to eBuy. Click here for more details on how you can prepare for this change.

Vendor Portal

Attention eMod users! We are making improvements to eMod based on your feedback. As of February 25th, you will be able to download your awarded eOffers from eMods. Additionally, your withdrawn and rejected eMods that are more than one (1) day old are moving to the eMod "Completed/History" section.
